WHO WE ARE

We are the staff of Adventure Surfers and we are happy to accommodate you. We have been living in the island for so long and we have always known the potential and beauty of Siargao and we want everyone to experience the beauty and adventure that it will offer. We are a group of friends who loves--guess what--adventure! We are locals who wants to boost the tourism of our small island. Surfing is our hobby and anyone who wants to try are absolutely welcome in the Surfing Capital of the Philippines. We take pride in the captivating scenery of the beaches and the tropical vibe that our island emits to give the best relaxing get away to our costumers!
