Overview

Purpose

To assist people especially in gaining more knowledge about surfing along with it's benefits, the surfing place, easy access to contact information for fast booking, and provides responses to FAQ. Audience

This website will mainly focus and cater on teens to abled surfers who want to have an adventure surfing the waves especially those who love water sports. First time surfers who want to learn about surfing would definitely enjoy it.

Content

Home Page

This page will serve as the advertising part of the website. It will serve as the eye-catching convincing part to lure in potential customers. I believe that a good design of a website attracts people to explore and know more about the website. As someone who loves design, it creates a strong first impression and displays credibility. It will also outshine other competitors of a similar content kind of website as it will incorporate latest trend designs of the year. This page will also contain the basic fun facts and all knowledge pertaining to white water rafting. The images will be designed interactively to fit in the fun facts written. It will give them information that will help them understand the basic terms as well as instructions for safety procedures. It will also contain some listed benefits of white water rafting to persuade them to try it. The design target are late teens to adults who are able in health and body to face the waves of rafting. It will also have the things they need to bring, requirements to qualify, and safety guidelines for it. All these things will be in this webpage but it will be designed in a fun way that it won’t be boring and information overload.

Images for the Home page

[Booking]

The booking part of this website will give them the instructions how to book a session. They will be able to see the available schedule: any date and time that suits them especially for those who wants to plan ahead. People particularly those who are the target audience of this website are usually busy but they can access this quickly and go on an adventure they will surely enjoy. We can’t afford to spoil the excitement by the complication of booking and design. We want this page to be as comprehensible as possible. Sales and promos will also be displayed to attract them more into booking bulk sessions that the costumers will surely enjoy. The prices will also be up for display so the customers can decide which amount will fit their budget while having quality adventure. It will require them to enter their email, contact number, and the booking session they chose but it will still include contact details if they want to talk in real-time to book. It is very convenient that bookings can be as easy with just one click they can already avail it without calling or going to the place. The design for this will be simple and efficient but still eye-catching so that they will not get lost while trying to navigate and do the next step for booking. It is also aimed that they can easily cancel or add any sessions anytime to what they have already decided.

Images for the Page 2

[About Us & FAQ]

This particular page of the website will focus on the information about the creators of the website as well as the professional email address of the white rafting company specifically those who have questions. This is a dual purpose page as well because it will also serve to answer frequently asked questions (FAQ) of customers which will range from additional information about booking, website failures, cancellations, and complaints etc. This website will be open twenty-four (24) hours a day to accommodate any customer and a response will be immediately sent within twelve hours of send. Not only does an FAQ with links to related content provide the user with a deeper experience of your website, it also boosts the internal link-building, which is an important search ranking factor. We are going to keep FAQ answers concise and include links to more in-depth information elsewhere on your site.
